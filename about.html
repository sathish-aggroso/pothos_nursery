Our Story

Nurturing plants and people since 2015

Growing from Love

Pothos Nursery began as a small passion project in our backyard greenhouse. What started as a love for nurturing tiny seedlings has blossomed into a beloved community destination for plant enthusiasts of all levels.

We believe every space deserves a touch of green, and every person deserves a plant that thrives in their care. That's why we carefully curate our collection to ensure each plant is healthy, vibrant, and suited to your unique environment.

Today, our nursery spans 5,000 square feet of greenhouse space and serves thousands of happy plant parents across the region.
